📘 Quick Tutorial: Using PromptForge

🎛 Dropdowns & Randomization

Each category (pose, expression, lighting, etc.) lets you choose manually or press REROLL to randomize.

💡 Tip: Rerolling multiple times creates more varied and powerful prompt strings.

🧠 Prompt Output

The main prompt box displays the combined result of all selected elements. It's updated live.

💡 Tip: Tap anywhere inside to copy, or hit "COPY PROMPT" to store to clipboard instantly.

🎨 Image Generation

Coming soon: hook in your favorite AI tools like OpenArt, RealVis, or Stable Diffusion backends.

💡 Tip: If you have an API key or local model path, you’ll be able to generate directly inside PromptForge.

🔞 NSFW Filters

Enable "Dirty Mode" to unlock extreme content categories, erotic expressions, and extra sliders.

💡 Tip: Be responsible and safe with what you create. Not everything should be shared.

💾 Saving & Watermarks

Pro users can save prompts, export images, and apply a custom watermark automatically.

💡 Tip: Enable watermark in settings to prevent your art from being stolen.

📍 Help Buttons (Micro)

Tiny ❓ icons appear next to advanced features. Tap for instant micro help!

💡 Tip: These will explain sliders, logic, and modifiers without cluttering your screen.
