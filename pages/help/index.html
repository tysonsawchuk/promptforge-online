💡 PromptForge Micro Help

Need a hand navigating the chaos? You're not alone. This help page explains what some of the cryptic buttons and features actually do.

💻 Interface Tips

🔘 “Generate Prompt”: Builds a full prompt from all selected dropdowns. Must be clicked after choosing your categories.
🎲 “Reroll Monster”: Rebuilds the massive side-prompt with new flavor without touching dropdown values.
📋 “Copy Prompt”: Instantly copies the final generated result to clipboard for use in OpenArt, etc.

🤖 Monster Prompt Logic

The Monster Prompt uses a layered percentage system with intelligent randomness. It adapts to your selections and adds dark, sexy, weird flavor automatically.

📌 Tooltips

Many buttons have small “?” help dots coming soon beside them. Hover or tap for quick micro-hints like this:

❓ “CFG Scale?” – Controls how tightly the AI follows your prompt. Higher = more precision, lower = more freedom.
