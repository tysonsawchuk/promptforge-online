How to use

1) Click Enable Camera (selfie) or Load Image (file).
2) Watch the “movie scanner” overlay; toggle Show Guides for feature vectors.
3) Switch tabs to get Prompt, Descriptor, Measurements, JSON, or Palette.
4) Use Copy / Export JSON / Snapshot to save your output.

Notes

• Everything runs on-device. No uploads. Best in good light ~0.5–1.0 m from camera.
• Measurements are per-frame (px) and normalized ratios; shapes are deterministic thresholds.
• SFW on this page. 18+ tools live in /labs (noindex, no ads).
