Download & Set Up Any AI Assistant — Alexa, Siri, Google, Gemini, ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Replika & More

Alexa (Android) Alexa (iOS) Google Assistant (Android/Web) Google Assistant (iOS) Siri (iOS Only) Gemini (Web/Android) ChatGPT (Web/iOS/Android) Claude (Web) Copilot (Web/Windows) Replika (Web/Android/iOS) Perplexity (Web/iOS/Android)
Alexa (Android/iOS):
1. Download from Google Play or App Store.
2. Open the app and sign in with your Amazon account.
3. Say “Alexa” or tap the mic to start.
Trouble? Restart phone, reinstall, check mic permissions, or sign out/in again.
Google Assistant (Android/iOS):
1. Download from Google Play or App Store.
2. Open, follow setup steps, grant permissions.
3. Say “Hey Google” or hold Home button.
Trouble? Update app, check language/mic settings, clear cache/data in Settings.
Siri (iOS Only):
1. Open Settings, tap “Siri & Search.”
2. Enable “Listen for Hey Siri.”
3. Hold Side/Home button to launch.
Trouble? Make sure Siri is ON, check network, or restart device.
Gemini (Google AI):
1. Go to gemini.google.com or get the Gemini app (Android only).
2. Log in with your Google account.
3. Start chatting or use voice input.
Trouble? Try a different browser, clear cache, or check account region.
ChatGPT (Web/App):
1. Visit chat.openai.com or download the ChatGPT app.
2. Log in or create a free OpenAI account.
3. Start a new chat and go wild.
Trouble? Refresh, clear cookies, or try a different browser/device.
Claude (Anthropic):
1. Visit claude.ai in your browser.
